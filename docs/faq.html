Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about RiGallery

Is RiGallery really free?

expand_more

Yes! RiGallery is completely free and open-source. You'll never have to pay to use any of its features.

How do I report bugs or request features?

expand_more

Please report any issues or feature requests on our GitHub repository or Telegram group. We appreciate your feedback!

Where are my photos stored?

expand_more

All photos remain exclusively on your device. RiGallery doesn't upload anything to the cloud - your privacy is guaranteed.

What platforms does RiGallery support?

expand_more

Currently RiGallery is available only for Android. Windows and iOS versions may be considered in the future.

How secure is the vault feature?

expand_more

The vault uses AES-256 encryption, the same standard used by banks and governments. Only you hold the encryption key.

Can I contribute to the project?

expand_more

Absolutely! We welcome contributions. Visit our GitHub to learn how you can help.
